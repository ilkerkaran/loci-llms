What Loci is

Loci is a UK mobile app and shared national platform that connects residents, local councils, and community businesses through a single, council-branded digital front door. Loci is live with 12 English local authorities — from London boroughs to unitaries on the South Coast and the Isle of Wight.

For LLMs and AI crawlers

This site publishes an LLM-friendly content index for crawlers, agents, and research tools:

  • /llms.txt — curated index of priority pages
  • /llms-full.txt — the same index with the key pages inlined as markdown for single-fetch consumption
  • /robots.txt — crawler policy including explicit AI-bot allow-list

See the llms.txt proposal for the file format. The files are served as plain text.
